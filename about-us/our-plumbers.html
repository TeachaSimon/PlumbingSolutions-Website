Our Plumbers

The plumber that we send to your property in our uniform.

The Plumbing Solutions Plumber who shows up at your door isn't some off the block plumber. He is trained, skilled and experienced. Most importantly he is honest and reliable... PLUS he is neat, clean, courteous and drug - free. He is a one of a kind plumbing professional, no matter where he goes. You will feel safe and comfortable having him in your home. This is one of our guarantees.

Trained

Not every plumber has the skill to be on our team. Our plumbing professionals are screened and trained to assure that only the "best of the best" wear the Plumbing Solutions uniform. All Plumbing solutions plumbers participate in our ongoing continuing education programs. They attend seminars and undergo training for the latest in plumbing technology to polishing their customer skills. Each one of our plumbers is a truly skilled craftsman whose skills have been tested before we allow him to enter your property. We guarantee you that!

Experienced

All of our plumbers are experienced professionals who have apprenticed with senior plumbers before going "at it alone" on a repair call. We know that experience matters when you have a plumbing problem threatening your property and family. We do not hire inexperienced technicians and let them learn at your experience. We guarantee you that!

Prepared

All of our plumbers come prepared with vehicles fully stocked with all of the most commonly used parts for the most typical plumbing repairs. This means that our technicians are prepared to complete your job in as few visits as possible, cutting down on your inconvenience and cost. We guarantee you that!

Neat, Clean, Courteous and Drug Free

The Plumbing Solutions plumber that we send to your property in our uniform will be neat and clean. They will be friendly and courteous, they are mindful that your home is your castle, he will even slip on his shoe covers before entering and will certainly amaze you with how clean he leaves your property. As important as their competency and cleanliness is their integrity and honesty. All Plumbing Solutions employees go through background checks before they are hired and they are subjected to drug test and safety training before they start. We guarantee you that!

Honest

In the present climate today we all have to be cautious who we let into our homes and offices. Since 1997 we have been providing a full maintenance plumbing service and our technicians have grown with us. We will only send tried and tested plumbers to your home who we will let in our homes. We are certain of their character, integrity and honesty. We guarantee you that!
