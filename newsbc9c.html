World Plumbing Day – March 11th, 2013

Plumbing Solutions will be yet again marking the observance of World Plumbing Day – March 11, 2013 with a series of events, such as the school outreach programme which includes the international and local poster competitions. Out of the the 81 winning poster submissions worldwide, Jordan Weekes of Bishop Anstey Junior School, Trinidad placed second in the World Plumbing Council’s international poster design competition. Jordan was also the winner of the Plumbing Solutions’ local poster design competition, while Frances Moultet of St. Andrew’s School placed second.

At the launch of the World Plumbing Day in Trinidad on March 9, 2012, Managing Director of Plumbing Solutions, Mr. Scott Fabres noted: “That it is the intention of Plumbing Solutions to improve the standards and perception of the plumbing industry in Trinidad and Tobago, and the wider Caribbean. Plumbing Solutions takes water efficiency and preservation seriously and promotes it to our consumers, within their homes and businesses. We remain steadfast and committed in our role towards the health of our nation.”

Please check back later for information on upcoming events and activities for World Plumbing Day, March 11th, 2013.

World Plumbing Day – March 11th, every year, everywhere!

2012 Winning Poster designed by Jordan Weekes, Grade 4, Bishop Anstey Junior School, Trinidad.

Congratulations to Jordan Weekes

Second Place Winner of the World Plumbing Council’s International Design Poster Competition and First Place Winner of Plumbing Solutions’ National Design Poster Competition.

As part of its school outreach programme, Plumbing Solutions extended an invitation to students from private primary schools in Trinidad to participate in the World Plumbing Council’s International Design Poster Competition. The posters were to be judged primarily for the message that World Plumbing Day represents – That Plumbing is vital to good health; the role that plumbing plays in our everyday life and the role that plumbing plays in the environmental issues. The artistic merit of the posters was secondary. The 81 posters were viewed by the staff at the American Society of Sanitary Engineering to choose the 10 best posters. These top ten posters were then put on display at the ASSE Mid-Year Board meetings for judging by the members present at the meeting.

We are pleased to announce that Jordan Weekes of Bishop Anstey Junior School received second place in the World Plumbing Council’s International Design Poster Competition out of 81 entries, and first place in Plumbing Solutions’ National Poster Design Competition.

On May 3rd, 2012 Plumbing Solutions Management and Staff attended the Bishop Anstey Junior School Assembly and presented to Jordan Weekes and his Principal, Ms. Tina Schuler prizes for his winning poster. Jordan received from the World Plumbing Council, $50 US Dollars, and his school received $500 US Dollars. Additionally, Plumbing Solutions presented to Jordan, $500 TT Dollars, and his school received $10,000 TT Dollars in plumbing supplies/services for the winning entry.

Plumbing Solutions congratulates Jordan Weekes and Bishop Anstey Junior School. We would like to thank all Teachers and Students for their participation in the design poster competition, and to those who were unable to participate in 2012 we look forward to your involvement in 2013.
